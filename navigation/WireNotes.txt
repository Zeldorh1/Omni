HOW TO APPLY THIS THEME ADD-ON (keeps your v2 structure)
-------------------------------------------------------
1) Drop the files under client/src/* into your project.
2) Register the new screens (non-breaking):
    import HomeStyled from '@/screens/HomeStyled';
    import HairHealthResultGood from '@/screens/HairHealthResultGood';
    import HairHealthResultRecovery from '@/screens/HairHealthResultRecovery';
    // In your Stack.Navigator (add, don't replace)
    <Stack.Screen name="HomeStyled" component={HomeStyled} options={{ title: 'Home' }} />
    <Stack.Screen name="HairGood" component={HairHealthResultGood} options={{ title: 'Hair Health' }} />
    <Stack.Screen name="HairRecovery" component={HairHealthResultRecovery} options={{ title: 'Hair Health' }} />
3) Use <PremiumDrawer visible={isOpen} onClose={()=>setOpen(false)} nav={navigation} /> in your Menu/Premium screen to show the dark drawer overlay like the screenshot.
4) Replace placeholder tile images in client/assets/images/explore*.png with your art (optional).
