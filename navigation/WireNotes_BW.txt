BLACK & WHITE THEME PATCH — Wiring Notes (keeps your current structure)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
1) Wrap your NavigationContainer with ThemeProvider in App.tsx:
     import { ThemeProvider } from '@/context/ThemeContext';
     // ...
     <ThemeProvider>
       <NavigationContainer>{/* existing tabs/stacks */}</NavigationContainer>
     </ThemeProvider>

2) Register these optional screens (add, don't replace):
     import HomeStyled_BW from '@/screens/HomeStyled_BW';
     import SettingsTheme from '@/screens/SettingsTheme';
     <Stack.Screen name="HomeBW" component={HomeStyled_BW} options={{ title: 'Home' }} />
     <Stack.Screen name="SettingsTheme" component={SettingsTheme} options={{ title: 'Appearance' }} />

3) Default look is crisp black/white. To enable your pink background like in earlier builds,
   navigate to Settings → Appearance and toggle "Colorful Backgrounds" ON.
